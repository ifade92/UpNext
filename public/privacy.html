Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how UpNext ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information provided by customers who use the UpNext barbershop queue management system operated by Fademasters Barbershop.

Information We Collect

When you check in to our walk-in queue, we collect the following information:

  • Your first name
  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your selected barber and service
  • Your check-in time and queue position

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to manage your place in the barbershop queue. Specifically, we use your phone number to send you SMS notifications including:

  • A confirmation that you have been added to the queue
  • A notification when you are approximately 2 positions away from your barber
  • A notification when it is your turn

We do not use your information for marketing purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to deliver SMS messages (via Twilio, our SMS service provider).

SMS Messaging & Opt-Out

By providing your phone number during check-in, you consent to receive SMS queue notifications for that visit only. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. After opting out, you will not receive further messages.

Data Retention

Your check-in information is stored temporarily and used only for the duration of your visit. Queue data is cleared regularly. We do not retain your phone number for future marketing or outreach.

Data Security

We use Firebase (Google) for data storage, which employs industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Access to customer data is restricted to authorized shop staff only.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate UpNext:

  • Firebase (Google) — database and authentication
  • Twilio — SMS message delivery

These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
