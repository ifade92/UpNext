05 Usage Rules
✓ Do
Use Deep Green as the primary background
Pair Outfit for headings with DM Sans for body
Use Accent Green sparingly for CTAs and key highlights
Use Grey-Green (#8BA898) for secondary text and "Next" wordmark
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the dot mark
✗ Don't
Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
Don't stretch, rotate, or modify the logo mark
Don't use more than 2 brand colors in a single UI element
Don't use Accent Green as a background color at large scale
Don't separate "Up" and "Next" — they are one word mark
